Terms & Conditions

Tour Cancellations
The tour will proceed in light rain, but may be canceled depending on weather conditions. If the tour is canceled by the organizer, the participation fee will be fully refunded.
Risks of Mountain Trekking
This tour involves walking on mountain trails for about an hour. In the unlikely event of an injury or accident during the tour, the guide will promptly provide first aid and take the best possible actions, including requesting emergency services if necessary. However, please note that we cannot assume any further liability (such as medical expenses or compensation for damages).
We kindly ask that you understand and accept these inherent risks of nature before participating.
Participation Requirements
The following individuals may not participate in the tour:
・Those who are pregnant
・Those under the influence of alcohol
・Those who have been advised by a doctor to avoid strenuous exercise
・Those who cannot follow the guide's instructions for safety reasons
